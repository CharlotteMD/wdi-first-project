Time

Level

The aim of the game is to match the mugshot with the image that you saw on the CCTV camera by selecting the correct facial features from the dropdown menu.

You will see the suspect on the CCTV camera for 4 seconds. You need to memorise the suspect's facial features before the screen goes blank.

You will then need to draw your mugshot by selecting the correct facial features from the drop down list that will appear on the mugshot.

When you think your mugshot matches the suspect that you saw on the CCTV camera, press the “Accuse” button.

If you are correct, the suspect will be sent to prison! If you are wrong, you will allow the suspect to escape!

You need to imprison as many villains as you can before the time is up!

You will see the time countdown in the stopwatch at the top of the screen. Below that, the timer will also keep score.
